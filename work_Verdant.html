Game Game Jam Remastered
Verdant Sailor is a top-down sailing game where you journey across a dying world, delivering Seeds of Life to restore what was lost. Battle corrupted sea creatures, establish trade routes, build your fortune, and upgrade your vessel as you venture deeper into dangerous waters. Originally developed as a submission for a 2024 Game Jam, this project was created over the course of approximately less than a month. Two years later, We revisited and remastered the game, driven by both nostalgia and the opportunity to expand upon the original vision by implementing features and assets that were cut during the Game Jam due to time constraints.

Clips from the YouTube Trailer, available to watch here: Verdant Sailor - Official Trailer

The remaster significantly expands the original experience under the new title, "Verdant Sailor: Full Voyage." True to its name, this edition restores and completes the content originally envisioned during the 2024 development, bringing the project closer to its intended scope. Despite the additional development and polish, the game remains completely free to play, reflecting our commitment to sharing it as a genuine passion project and a labor of love. Full Buoyancy & Water Physics One of the spotlight feature this game has is full buoyancy and water physics, unlike just flat out sea plane, this game features a 3D procedural moving water waves. Complete with a stylized shader accompanying the beautiful 3D water.
We achieved this effect by combining multiple sine waves to emulate the fluidity of ocean waves. Then it is put into a function that offsets the vertices on the horizontal axis to form sharper peaks, this process turns it into gerstner waves. While not a physically accurate fluid simulation, this approach recreates the natural rise and fall of ocean waves with minimal computational cost. Although it doesn't significantly impact the gameplay, the dynamic ocean surface improves the experience to be more immersive than just sailing on a flat sea plane Gameplay Loop You are entrusted with delivering the Seed of Life to cleanse a total of five islands. Each island you restore unlocks new trade routes, granting access to increasingly rare and exotic spices. As your journey progresses, you'll expand your trading network, grow your wealth, and build a thriving maritime enterprise while bringing life back to the archipelago.
The corrupted seas are far from safe, with dangerous creatures lurking beneath the waves. Throughout your voyage, you'll encounter hostile enemies that will stop at nothing to sink your vessel. Fortunately, your ship is equipped with powerful cannons, allowing you to defend yourself and overcome the threats that stand between you and your destination.
The Tainted-Ray is also a new addition to the Full Voyage edition, giving more variation to the enemies present in the GameJam Release. More 'Full Voyage' Features One of the features absent from the original Game Jam release was a proper day-and-night cycle. I implemented a dynamic lighting system that transitions seamlessly between day and night, while the islands emit baked lighting after sunset to create a warmer, more atmospheric environment. To help players keep track of the passage of time, I also designed a simple time indicator. Rather than displaying a conventional clock, it communicates the current time of day through descriptive names, making the game feels more immersive. As a final tribute to the project, beside countless of bug fixings we gave the game the ending it always deserved. Upon successfully cleansing all five islands, players unlock an entirely new final boss encounter, providing a climactic conclusion that was originally planned but could not be realized during the Game Jam. This addition completes the experience and brings the original vision to life. And to let players prepare their vessel to confront the new Goblin Leviathan, I introduced an unlockable boatsmith who becomes available as the game progresses. Allowing players to upgrade their boat so it can withstand the Leviathan's attacks.
The game is available free to play on Itch.io, grab yourself a copy, set sail, and embark on your journey across the seas to restore the islands and uncover the adventure that awaits.
