Introduction
Hello my name is Kevan Kaneakram and im a Game Developer and Graphic Designer born and raised in Bandung, Indonesia. Growing up, I was the kind of kid who could spend hours creating anything. Whether it was drawing on paper, tinkering on a computer, building with LEGO, taking photographs, or even making short films for a while, I was always drawn to the process of creating. Looking back, I think game development has always been at the back of my mind. As a kid, I would create my own games entirely on paper, sketching interfaces, inventing mechanics, and dreaming up worlds of my own. Long before I knew how to actually make games, way long before I learned how to code. That same creative curiosity naturally found its way into graphic design. I love illustrating and crafting memorable brand identities. Beyond imagining games of my own, I also found myself reimagining existing brands and redesigning their packaging, simply because I enjoyed exploring how things could be made more distinctive and meaningful.

Services
Creative Direction Developing visual concepts and directing their execution across graphics, photography, video, and advertising. From concept to execution, I make sure every element works together with purpose.
Graphic Design & Illustration Creating visuals that communicate clearly and leave a lasting impression. Whether it's illustrations, promotional materials, or digital assets, every piece is crafted with intention.
Brand Design / Redesign Building brands with memorable identities, or refining existing ones without losing what makes them recognizable. A strong brand is more than just a logo—it's a story people can remember.
3D Modelling & Texturing Designing stylized and game-ready 3D assets with attention to form, material, and presentation. Every model is built to support both aesthetics and functionality.
UI/UX Design Designing interfaces that are intuitive, functional, and visually engaging. Great user experiences happen when usability and aesthetics work hand in hand.
Video Editing & Motion Design Bringing visuals to life through editing, motion, and rhythm. Whether it's a cinematic sequence or a motion graphic, timing and purpose are at the core of every frame.
Web Development Bringing ideas to the web through thoughtful design and clean implementation. I build responsive websites that prioritize clarity, usability, and a cohesive visual experience.
" Aren't you a Game Developer? Why don't you offer game development related services? "
Game development isn't a service I actively offer—it's where many of my disciplines come together. It's the medium through which I express my ideas, combining design, programming, art, storytelling, and direction into a single experience. Most of that work happens within my own studio alongside my team, where I lead each project from the ground up. While certain disciplines, such as additional 3D assets or sound, are collaborative efforts, the overall creative vision and the majority of the development are mostly driven by me. Game development is featured throughout this portfolio not because it's a service I sell, but because it's the strongest representation of how I think, create, and solve problems. Every discipline I work in ultimately feeds back into that process. That said, I'm always open to a conversation. If you believe your project aligns with what I value or you're looking to create something truly meaningful together, I'd love to hear about it.
