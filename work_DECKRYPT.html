DECKRYPT
2026
Game Independent Project Ongoing
This project is still ongoing so expect some changes in the future. DECKRYPT is a roguelike RPG that blends strategic deckbuilding with the narrative depth of a Dungeons & Dragons campaign. Play as a captive forced into the Dungeon Master's twisted game, journey through his world, and fight for your freedom by conquering every challenge he sets before you. RPGs and roguelikes have always been my favorite genres. I enjoy the rewarding progression of strengthening your character through better gear, as well as the fresh experience every run in a roguelike provides because of its replayability. DECKRYPT was inspired by bringing those two experiences together.
Dungeons & Dragons also heavily influenced DECKRYPT's narrative. I wanted players to experience the same sense of imagination that comes from listening to a Dungeon Master tell a story, allowing each scene and encounter to come to life in their minds.
The world is filled with diverse adventures and locations, each offering its own opportunities and challenges. The path you take is entirely up to you, venture through the Dungeon Master's story and forge your own adventure.
This project also gave me the opportunity to explore pixel art. From the card illustrations to the environmental textures, I experimented with pairing hand-crafted pixel art with stylized 3D environments to create an eerie yet solid visual style.
