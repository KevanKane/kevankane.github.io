DECKRYPT Illustration Ongoing

Individually illustrated cards developed for my upcoming roguelike RPG DECKRYPT.

Action Cards These action cards represent the player's action as the name suggests, Attack, Enhance, Hinder, and Restore. Inspired by traditional poker playing cards, they feature a mirrored layout that maintains a consistent appearance when viewed from either the player's or the opponent's perspective.
Equipment Cards For the Equipment Cards, I took a more freeform approach since they are permanently displayed on the right side of the player's table, representing the player's equipped items, similar to equipment in a Dungeons & Dragons game. This also helps establish a clear visual distinction between the Equipment Cards and the Action Cards.
