Saltwater Creatures
2025
College Typography Illustration
Whales sing in the deep.
Crabs dreams drift with tides.
Salt clings to my skin. This collection of typographic artworks was created as part of a Typography course assignment. The project explores the concept of Animal Typography, where words, letters, or entire alphabets are arranged to form the silhouette of an animal. For this series, I focused on saltwater creatures. I chose saltwater creatures as the theme because of my long-standing fascination with the deep sea. Since childhood, I've been captivated by marine life, especially sharks, whales, and the unknown creatures that lurks deep within.
