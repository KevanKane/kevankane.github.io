About Me
Kevan Kaneakram '06 Game Developer & Designer
I've been making things for as long as I can remember, turning ideas into interactive experiences, whether that's through line of codes, design, or art. Game development has been my primary focus since I was in elementary school, driven by countless hours spent playing games and imagining how I would design them myself. Along the way, I've also developed a strong foundation in visual design, helping shape everything from interfaces and branding to the overall player experience. Im a firm believer that reason is very important for those who hold privileges. So every line of code and every design decision needs to have their reason to exist.
