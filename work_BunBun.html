BunBun Bakery
2026
Brand Design Visual Design College
BunBun Bakery & Coffee is a cafe located in Tirtasari, West Bandung, offering a variety of coffee, freshly baked bread, pastries, cheesecakes, and casual dining options.
This project reimagines BunBun's visual identity while preserving its core brand recognition. The original blue color palette was retained and refined with a bolder shade, creating a more contemporary look while maintaining the brand's familiar character.
The typography was modernized by shifting from a vintage-inspired sans serif style to a more elegant serif direction, balancing a timeless aesthetic with a fresh, updated appearance. Supporting typography uses Urbanist, pairing ExtraBold and Regular weights to create a clear visual hierarchy and a relaxed, welcoming tone.
Furthermore, I developed a supergraphic inspired by the bread tag usually found on a bread packaging. The repeating pattern reflects the production process and creates a rhythmic visual system that is both distinctive and closely tied to the brand. Design Implementation Example (Mockup)
