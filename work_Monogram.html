Monogram Study
2025
College Typography Illustration
This logo was developed during a Typography course assignment. This project explored the integration of letterforms into a cohesive mark while emphasizing readability, balance, and visual impact. Its a visual representation of my initials, crafted with a strong emphasis on geometry, minimalism, and structure. My approach to this design was deeply influenced by my love for mathematics and symmetry, which is reflected in the precise and well-balanced composition.
This logo is not just a monogram—it’s a reflection of my identity, my passion for structured design, and my appreciation for mathematical harmony.
